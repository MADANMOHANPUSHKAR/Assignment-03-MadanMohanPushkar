🍗 Butter Chicken

Rich, creamy tomato-based curry with tender chicken, aromatic spices, and a velvety butter finish.

← Back to All Recipes

20 min 30 min 4 Medium Non-Veg

About This Dish

Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is one of the most popular Indian dishes worldwide. Originating from Delhi in the 1950s, this iconic recipe features succulent pieces of marinated chicken simmered in a lusciously rich, tomato-based sauce enriched with butter, cream, and a blend of warming spices. It pairs beautifully with naan bread or steamed basmati rice.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 400g tomato purée
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 100ml heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces. Combine yogurt, lemon juice, turmeric, chili powder, and garam masala in a bowl. Add chicken, mix well, cover, and marinate for at least 1 hour (overnight is best).
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a heavy pan over medium-high heat. Cook marinated chicken in batches until lightly charred, about 3–4 minutes per batch. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, melt 2 tablespoons of butter. Sauté finely chopped onions until golden brown (6–8 minutes). Add minced garlic and grated ginger, cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add tomato purée along with chili powder, coriander powder, and a pinch of sugar. Cook on medium heat for 12–15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until oil separates from the masala.
  5. Reduce heat to low. Add cooked chicken back into the sauce. Stir in heavy cream and honey. Simmer gently for 10–12 minutes, allowing flavors to meld.
  6. Finish by stirring in 2 tablespoons of cold butter and crushed kasuri methi. Season with salt. Garnish with a swirl of cream and fresh cilantro leaves.
